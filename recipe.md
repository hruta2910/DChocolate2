Dark chocolate & Peanut butte cups

First melt the dark chocolate and put 1/4 coconut oil in it.
Mix peanut butter, honey and vanilla extract.(According to how much you want to make)
Pour a layer of dark chocolate in a butter cupcake paper.
Let it harden.
Put a layer of peanut butter mixure on top of the dark chocolate.
After the peanut butter is settled, pour a layer of dark chocolate
 on top and let it harden.
